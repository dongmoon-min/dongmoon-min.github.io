Biography

I'm currently an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SKKU.

Before joining SKKU, I was post-doc in ASRI at Seoul National University.
I earned Ph.D. and BS degree at Seoul National University under the supervision of Prof Jangwoo Kim.

I mainly have worked on (1) designing scalable quantum computer systems and (2) superfast server architectures running at cryogenic temperatures (-196℃ ~ -269℃).
I also have conducted several research on (3) computer performance modeling and simulation, (4) processor microarchitecture, (5) analog-based process-in-memory architecture for AI, and (6) cost-efficient datacenter architectures.
I'm looking for passionate research interns, Ph.D. students, and Postdocs to join the team. Please email me if you are interested in.
